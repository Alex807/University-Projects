frog
Definition: A small tailless amphibian of the order Anura that typically hops.
Example: The frog's croak echoed across the pond
Synonyms: toad, newt, polliwog
Antonyms: giant, mammal, bird

car
Definition: A wheeled vehicle that moves independently, with at least three wheels, powered mechanically, steered by a driver and mostly for personal transportation.
Example: car is a vehicle for transport
Synonyms: automobile, motorcar, auto
Antonyms: pedestrian, walker, cyclist

cat
Definition: An animal of the family Felidae:
Example: The cat chased the mouse under the table
Synonyms: pet, feline, puss
Antonyms: dog, canine, adversary
